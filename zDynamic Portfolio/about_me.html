A bit about me

Hello! I am a third year student at The University of Colorado Boulder studying creative technology and design with a minor in dance. I'm passionate about design and exploring ways to express creativity, from graphic design to drawing, painting and even animation. I enjoy exploring different ways to bring ideas to life through different mediums whether it's digital or tactile.

During my time at CU, I have learned about design thinking and how to blend creativity with technology. I am excited to continue growing as a designer and keep expanding my skill set.

Outside of design, I’m passionate about dance and have trained in ballet, tap, jazz, and contemporary. Dance allows me to express my creativity, whether it’s in choreography or through movement.

  • Exploring different design styles
  • Storytelling through visual design
  • Gragic Design and Branding
  • Working on creative projects with others
  • Always learning and improving my skills
